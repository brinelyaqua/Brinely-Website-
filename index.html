SPF (Specific Pathogen Free) means the broodstock used to produce the PL was certified free of known shrimp diseases. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) testing confirms whether pathogens like WSSV are present in the PL batch itself. Both certifications together are the strongest guarantee of disease-free seed — and we verify both before recommending any hatchery.
