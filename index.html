The Problem

The shrimp seed market is broken for farmers

Every crop season, thousands of farmers across India are cheated, misled, or simply unlucky — because they had no trusted advisor to guide their seed purchase.

🧑‍💼
Brokers with hidden agendas
Local seed brokers earn commissions from specific hatcheries. They push you toward whoever pays them more — not whoever has the best seed for your pond conditions.
⚠ Most brokers earn ₹0.50–₹2 per PL commission
🦠
No way to verify disease status
WSSV, EMS, and other diseases wipe out entire crops. But most farmers have no way to verify a hatchery's PCR test results or inspect biosecurity protocols before booking.
⚠ Disease causes 40–60% crop loss annually
📋
Zero transparency on quality
PLR data, survival history, broodstock origin — farmers almost never receive this before purchasing. You're buying blind in a market where quality varies wildly.
⚠ 3× quality difference between hatcheries

Brinely exists to fix this — completely free for farmers

We are not brokers. We are not affiliated with any single hatchery. We are trained aquaculture advisors who work exclusively in your interest — matching you to the right seed, from the right hatchery, at the right time.
